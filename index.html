About

Crystal Water Park started its journey from 2010 and spread across 14 acres of land dedicated to fun, food, and frolic. Crystal Water Park is biggest water park situated on the outskirts of Firozabad(Sailai, saati road). Firozabad is also proud to have Crystal Water Park biggest and beautifully landscaped Theme Park.

Management had assured to keep the ecological balance gardens with magnificent water bodies. Park maintains all the basic amenities like First-Aid, Lockers, Safety and Security norms to offera never-anding entertaining experience for all the tourists.
